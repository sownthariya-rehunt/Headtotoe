Welcome To

HEAD 2 TOE

Welcome to our HEAD 2 TOE physiotherapy clinic, where we have been providing exceptional care to our patients from 2009 and remembered as the best physiotherapy clinic in Coimbatore. Our clinic was founded by Dr.Prabu & Dr.Priya Prabu who had a passion for helping people recover from physical ailments, injuries and disabilities.

Our Founders noticed and realized that there is a high demand for qualified physiotherapy services in society which led them to open the HEAD 2 TOE physiotherapy clinic, where they could provide the best possible care to patients in Coimbatore.

Dr.Prabu graduated with his Bachelor of Physiotherapy from PSG College Of Physiotherapy (Coimbatore) in 2009 and completed his Master of Physiotherapy in Orthopaedics at The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University. Dr.Prabu is a pain specialist with vast experience (14+ years) in the pain management field.

He has served as a chief physiotherapist in Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan Central Government Scheme for paediatric disability children for five years.

He has enlightened his knowledge through many courses and obtained Certificates in below,

  • Advanced manual therapy techniques
  • Chiropractic practice
  • Osteopath manipulation
  • Cranial and visceral manipulation
  • Dry needling
  • Fascial manipulation
  • Myofascial trigger release
  • Mulligan mobilization
  • Kinesiology taping
  • IASTM (Instrument-Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization)
  • Cupping etc ..

His knowledge in Assessment, Diagnosis and treatment ensures the best patient care for both Sporting and Non-Sporting injuries and also both acute and chronic pain conditions.

His clinic has helped countless people over the 14 + years, and his impact on the community has been immeasurable. His commitment to providing the best possible care to his patients and his involvement in the community make him a true inspiration to others in the healthcare profession.

Dr.Priya Prabu graduated with her Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) from PSG College Of Physiotherapy (Coimbatore) in 2009 and Masters of Physiotherapy (MPT) in Orthopaedics under The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University.

She is a Co-Founder of Head 2 Toe physiotherapy clinic with 14+ years of hands-on experience in pain rehabilitation & women’s health care rehabilitation. Choosing the right women's health physiotherapist is an important decision that can have a significant impact on women’s health and well-being. At our clinic, we are proud to have one of the best women's health physiotherapists in the city.

She had specialized training and expertise in treating a wide range of conditions that affect women's health such as,

  • Pelvic floor dysfunction
  • PCOD
  • Menstrual pain
  • Urine leakage
  • Vaginismus
  • Sexual pain
  • Infertility
